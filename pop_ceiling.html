POP Ceiling

Stylish and Decorative False Ceiling Solution


About POP Ceiling

POP (Plaster of Paris) ceiling is a popular false ceiling solution used to create stylish and decorative interior designs. It is made from a special plaster material that can be molded into different shapes and patterns, giving ceilings a modern and attractive look.

POP ceilings are widely used in homes, offices, hotels, and commercial spaces. They help improve interior aesthetics while also allowing installation of decorative lighting, hidden LED lights, and creative ceiling designs.

  • Decorative and Customizable Ceiling Designs
  • Smooth and Elegant Interior Finish
  • Perfect for Modern Lighting Installations
  • Suitable for Homes and Commercial Spaces
